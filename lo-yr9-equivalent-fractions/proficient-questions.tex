{\LARGE \textbf{L02 Equivalent fractions}}\\[0.35em]
Find or identify fractions that are equivalent.

\vspace{2.6em}
{\LARGE \textbf{Proficient}}
\begin{multicols}{3}
\begin{enumerate}[
  itemsep=8.8em,
  label=\textbf{\arabic*.}, 
  labelwidth=2.2em,
  labelsep=0.75em,
  leftmargin=!,
  align=left
]
\item Write two different fractions equivalent to $\frac{3}{5}$.
\item Write two different fractions equivalent to $\frac{4}{7}$.
\item Fill in the blank: $\frac{5}{6} = \frac{\square}{18}$
\item Fill in the blank: $\frac{7}{8} = \frac{21}{\square}$
\item Fill in the blank: $\frac{9}{10} = \frac{\square}{50}$
\item Are $\frac{6}{9}$ and $\frac{10}{15}$ equivalent?
\item Are $\frac{8}{12}$ and $\frac{2}{3}$ equivalent?
\item Which fraction is equivalent to $\frac{2}{3}$: $\frac{6}{9}$, $\frac{4}{9}$, or $\frac{8}{15}$?
\item Which fraction is equivalent to $\frac{3}{4}$: $\frac{9}{12}$, $\frac{6}{10}$, or $\frac{12}{20}$?
\item Simplify \textbf{12/16} and state a fraction equivalent to it.
\end{enumerate}
\end{multicols}
