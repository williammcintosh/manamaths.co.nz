{\LARGE \textbf{L02 Equivalent fractions}}\\[0.35em]
Find or identify fractions that are equivalent.

\vspace{2.6em}
{\LARGE \textbf{Proficient}}
\begin{multicols}{3}
\begin{enumerate}[
  itemsep=7.4em,
  label=\textbf{\arabic*.}, 
  labelwidth=2.2em,
  labelsep=0.75em,
  leftmargin=!,
  align=left
]
\item Write two different fractions equivalent to \textbf{3/5}.
\item Write two different fractions equivalent to \textbf{4/7}.
\item Fill in the blank: \textbf{5/6 = }\rule{1.4cm}{0.15mm}\textbf{/18}
\item Fill in the blank: \textbf{7/8 = 21/}\rule{1.4cm}{0.15mm}
\item Fill in the blank: \textbf{9/10 = }\rule{1.4cm}{0.15mm}\textbf{/50}
\item Are \textbf{6/9} and \textbf{10/15} equivalent?
\item Are \textbf{8/12} and \textbf{2/3} equivalent?
\item Which fraction is equivalent to \textbf{2/3}: \textbf{6/9}, \textbf{4/9}, or \textbf{8/15}?
\item Which fraction is equivalent to \textbf{3/4}: \textbf{9/12}, \textbf{6/10}, or \textbf{12/20}?
\item Simplify \textbf{12/16} and state a fraction equivalent to it.
\end{enumerate}
\end{multicols}
