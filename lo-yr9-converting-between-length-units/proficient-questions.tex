{\LARGE \textbf{Converting between length units — Proficient}}
\begin{tasks}(3)
\task Convert 2.75 m to cm.
\task Convert 4800 mm to m.
\task Convert 0.65 km to m.
\task A skipping rope is 2.4 m long. Write its length in cm and mm.
\task A track lap is 400 m. What is this in km?
\task Order these from shortest to longest: 0.9 m, 95 cm, 980 mm.
\task A trail is 3.6 km long. Four friends each walk the full trail once. What total distance do they walk in metres?
\task A builder cuts 1.8 m from a 250 cm plank. How many centimetres remain?
\task A beetle is 18 mm long. How long would 25 beetles be end-to-end in cm?
\task A bus travels 12.5 km in the morning and 8700 m in the afternoon. What total distance is this in km?
\task Convert 0.045 km to cm.
\task Explain which operation you use to convert metres to kilometres, and why.
\end{tasks}
